Welcome to Central Station's Train Scheduler, Instruct.

While our train schedule is small, we transport nearly 65,000 citizens every day. Instruct keeps track of our train schedules and refreshes every minute to provide you with the most current information.

As new tracks and cars come online, please enter their information in the form below. Please note that all fields are required in order to submit a train's schedule to our database.
